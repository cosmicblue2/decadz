Hello, I'm Decadz

A passionate self-taught photographer with a love for nature, people, and untold moments. I believe every frame is a feeling captured in stillness. Through travel and daily life, I aim to freeze the soul of a moment — so it lasts forever.

Whether it's the calm of a misty morning or the burst of a laugh in a crowd, I love turning the everyday into something unforgettable.
